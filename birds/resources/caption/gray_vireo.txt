Adult. Small, stocky songbird with a round head. Plain gray overall with a paler belly. Note thin white eyering and small white line in front of the eye. 
Adult. Round-headed, drab gray vireo with a thin white eyering. Often flicks its tail side to side and is more active than other vireos. 
Adult. Song is a halting series of 2- and 3-note phrases with a burry quality. 
Juvenile. Juveniles are washed brown with a more distinct wingbar than adults. Note white eyering.
