Male. Song is a bubbling purring, followed by an ascending series of short, high notes.
Adults. Often feeds in mixed flocks of other blackbirds on the ground or in trees and shrubs. 
Habitat. Found in open or semiopen habitats, especially agricultural areas with patches of trees and shrubs.
