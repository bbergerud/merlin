Adult. Medium-sized sparrow with a striking face pattern: a white stripe above and below the eye and a black throat. 
Juvenile. Juveniles lack the black throat and are browner overall. Note pale eyebrow stripe. 
Adult. Plump, barrel-chested sparrow with a thick bill and striking head pattern. Call is a thin chip; song is a few introductory notes followed by trills.
