Juvenile. A delicate shorebird with a dovelike head and a short bill. Stands tall on its yellow legs. Juveniles are scaly brown above with darker spots on the breast. 
Adult/immature. Slender and delicate with a round dovelike head. Mottled brown above and plain buffy below.
Adult. Holding their wings in an outstretched position, males perform a unique mating display on leks to attract mates.
Adult. Small round head and short bill give it a dovelike appearance. Adults are buffy with less scaly above than juveniles. 
Adult. Underwings are gleaming white with a buffy bar at the top.
