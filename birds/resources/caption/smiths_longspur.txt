Breeding male. Medium-sized and sparrowlike with a thin bill. Breeding males have an unstreaked orangish belly and a patterned black-and-white face. Note white outer tail feathers. 
Nonbreeding. Nonbreeding birds are buffy below with fine streaking down the belly. 

Breeding female. Breeding females have a heavily streaked back often flecked with white. The face is rather plain with a white eyering and thin bill. 
Nonbreeding. Nonbreeding birds have two thin white wingbars, though the wingbars are not always visible. Note buffy underparts with fine streaky.
