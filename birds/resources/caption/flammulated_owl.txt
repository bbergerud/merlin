Gray morph. Small owl with short, feathered ear tufts and a short tail. Ear tufts often held flat. Dark vertical stripes or dashes down belly with varying amounts of rusty feathers. Note dark eyes. 
Red morph. Small owl with short ear tufts that are often held flat. Red morph individuals have more extensive rusty plumage.
Gray morph. Small owl that gives single-noted hoots that can often be difficult to locate.
