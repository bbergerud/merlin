Male. Large sea duck with an oblong head and a smaller bill. Males have an ornately colored blue, green, and orange head and a black-and-white body. Note orange bill. 
Female. Large sea duck. Females have a black bill and are mottled in brown and black. 
Male and female. Males display for females by pushing their head forward while swimming around them. 
Immature male. Large sea duck. Immature males have a pale orange bill, a white chest, and a brown back. 
Male with Common Eiders. Large sea duck with an oblong head and a smaller bill. Males have an ornately colored blue, green, and orange head and a black-and-white body. Often gathers with other sea ducks.
