One of the largest members of the Empidonax group. The forehead is rather flat, sloping gently up giving the head a peaked look. Olive above with 2 pale wingbars and a narrow eyering. 
Relatively large flycatcher with long wings. Olive above and yellowish below, with 2 pale wingbars, and a thin white eyering. 
Song is an explosive, two-parted "ker-chip!" or "peet-sah!"
Olive and yellowish flycatcher with a long and broad bill, especially noticeable from below. Some individuals appear more yellow below than others. 
Common flycatcher of deciduous forests often near water.
