Adult male. Somewhat hefty finchlike songbird with a thick bill. Males are and unmistakable blue and reddish purple overall, but can appear dark from a distance. 
Female. Females are pale brown overall with an unstreaked breast. 
Male. Males have a blue body marked with reddish purple chest and nape. Occurs in scrubby woodlands, thorn forests, areas near streams, and desert washes. 
Female. Females are tan overall with unmarked wings and a clean breast. Note the thick bill with a curved upper mandible.
