Breeding. Small, plump seabird with a short bill and tail and narrow wings. Breeding birds appear brown overall. 
Nonbreeding. Nonbreeding birds are dark blackish gray above and white below with a white collar around the neck. When resting on the water, the tail is often held up and out of the water. 
Nonbreeding. Often seen in small groups during the nonbreeding season. From a distance note white collar on a dark and white seabird. 
Nonbreeding. Plump, short-tailed seabird with a short bill. Note white collar and black eye patch.
