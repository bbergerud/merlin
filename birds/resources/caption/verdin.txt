Adult. Tiny desert bird with a yellow head and chestnut shoulder patches (not always visible). 
Juvenile. Juveniles are pale gray overall and lack the adult's yellow head and chestnut shoulder patches. The short bill is thicker at the base and finely pointed at the tip.
