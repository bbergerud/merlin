Adult male. Medium-sized woodpecker with a white face and crown. Males have a small red rear crown patch. 
Adult female. Appropriately named woodpecker with a white head. Females lack red crown patch. Back is solid black with two white patches in the wings. 
Immature. Immatures have a red patch in the middle of the crown instead of at the back of the head like adult males.
Adult male. Black woodpecker with a white head. White patches in the wing are not always visible on perched birds, but they are noticeable in flight.
