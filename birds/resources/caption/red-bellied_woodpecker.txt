Male. Medium-sized black-and-white barred woodpecker with a pale belly. Males have a red crown and nape. 
Female. Medium-sized black-and-white barred woodpecker with a pale belly. Females have a red nape, lacking the red crown. 
Adult male. Clings to tree bark and uses stiff tail feathers for support. Probes into bark for insects. Note white flashes in wings as this bird takes flight.
