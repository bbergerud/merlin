A tall, skinny sandpiper of grasslands with a thin neck and small head. Speckled brown overall with a short, thin bill. 
Slender with a short, thin bill and long neck. Mottled brown above with spotting on flanks and neck. Note big-eyed look. 
A terrestrial shorebird that often perches on fence posts in grasslands. 
Often lands on fence posts, raising its wings after landing. 
In flight the outer flight feather is white, appears as a sliver of white on the upper wing, which can be difficult to see.
