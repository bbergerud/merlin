Adult male. Large blackbird with a stout body, a large head, and a long, conical bill. Adult males have a bright yellow head and breast and a black body. Note white patch on wings, obvious in flight. 
Female. Large blackbird with a big head. Females are brown instead of black, with a duller yellow head and breast.
