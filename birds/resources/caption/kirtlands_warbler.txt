Male. Large steel gray-and-yellow warbler with white eye crescents. Gray above with black streaks. Throat and belly is yellow. Males have a dark mark in front of the eyes. 
Female. Females are grayish above with black streaks. They are pale yellow below with dark spotting. Note thin white eye crescents.
