Male. Small woodpecker with black-and-white barring up its back. Note that the barring on the back does not go all the way up the back, leaving a solid black patch below the neck. Males have a red patch at the rear of the head.
Female. Females look like males, but lack the red crown patch. Note thin facial stripes and white underparts.
