Small flycatcher with cinnamon underparts, a short bill, and a white eyering. 
Worn individuals only show a hint of cinnamon on the breast. Note small size, eyering, and short bill. 

Small, cinnamon washed flycatcher with a brownish back and 2 pale wingbars. Intensity of cinnamon wash varies among individuals. 
Breeds in open pine and oak forests.
