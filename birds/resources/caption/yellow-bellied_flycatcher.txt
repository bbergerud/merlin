Small, compact flycatcher. Yellowish olive overall with a strong white eyering and two whitish wingbars. 
Breeds in boreal evergreen forests and peatlands. Found in the understory of deciduous forests during migration. Note white eyering and yellow wash across belly. 

Small flycatcher with a slightly peaked head. Yellow-olive above and yellowish below, but some worn birds may lack yellow wash below.
Compact flycatcher with a white eyering and two white wingbars (but note that young birds can have yellowish wingbars).
